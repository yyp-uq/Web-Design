Ultrahand

Ultrahand allows Link to pick up, move, rotate, and seamlessly attach objects together. Using this powerful ability, he can craft bridges to cross chasms, assemble vehicles to explore vast terrains, and engineer complex contraptions to solve puzzles in creative ways. Whether navigating the skies or solving shrine challenges, Ultrahand opens up limitless possibilities for invention and exploration.

Fuse

Fuse allows Link to magically combine materials with his weapons, shields, or arrows to enhance durability, boost damage, or unlock unique effects. From attaching a boulder to a sword for crushing blows, to fusing elemental items for fire or ice attacks, the possibilities are endless. This mechanic encourages creativity—resulting in powerful synergies or even amusingly unexpected outcomes that add both strategy and fun to every encounter.

Ascend

Ascend introduces a whole new dimension to exploration by allowing Link to pass through solid objects above him. With this extraordinary ability, he can rise through ceilings, hillsides, caves, and even certain enemies—transforming vertical obstacles into seamless shortcuts. Whether escaping danger, scaling towers, or uncovering hidden areas, Ascend redefines traversal and opens up clever new paths that would otherwise be unreachable.

Recall

Recall allows Link to reverse the movement of objects through time, turning moments of chaos into clever opportunities. He can ride fallen boulders back into the sky, retrieve weapons he's thrown, or send enemy projectiles hurtling back toward their source. Whether solving intricate puzzles or turning the tide of battle, Recall offers a unique way to manipulate cause and effect in the world around him.
