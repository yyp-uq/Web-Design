Link is the brave protagonist of The Legend of Zelda series, chosen by destiny to wield the Master Sword and defend Hyrule. Though silent, his courage and resolve make him a timeless hero. He undertakes perilous quests to rescue Princess Zelda and confront evil, especially the dark lord Ganon.

Ganondorf is a powerful sorcerer and king of the Gerudo tribe who seeks the Triforce to gain ultimate power. Driven by ambition and darkness, he often returns in monstrous forms—most notably as Ganon—to plunge Hyrule into chaos. He is Link’s eternal rival and a symbol of corruption and imbalance.

Princess Zelda is the wise and compassionate ruler of Hyrule, deeply connected to the goddess Hylia. Far from a passive figure, she plays a vital role in resisting evil—sometimes through secret identities or powerful magic. Her enduring bond with Link is central to the fate of the kingdom.

Rauru is a sage and ancient protector of Hyrule, closely tied to the Temple of Time. In Tears of the Kingdom, he is revealed as one of Hyrule’s first kings and a founder of its legacy. Wise and noble, Rauru embodies the Zonai heritage and guides Link through the deeper mysteries of Hyrule’s past.

Mineru, elder sister of Rauru and Sage of Spirit, is known for her deep wisdom and mastery of an ancient mechanical construct. In the later stages, she guides Link in spiritual form and aids him in battle through fusion combat. As guardian of lost knowledge, she helps reveal the deeper truths of Hyrule’s past.

Sidon, once prince and later king of the Zora, wields powerful water-based abilities as the Sage of Water. A close friend of Link, he is a wise and compassionate leader. In times of crisis, he stands tall to protect his people, showing unwavering duty, strength, and leadership.

Riju is the young chief of the Gerudo tribe and the chosen Sage of Lightning. Although still in her youth, she demonstrates remarkable maturity and commanding presence. She can summon lightning strikes to devastate enemies and stands as a symbol of strength and hope for the new generation of the Gerudo people.

Tulin is a young warrior of the Rito tribe and son of Teba. He inherits the power of the Sage of Wind and commands strong gusts to aid Link in flight and battle. Though impulsive and overconfident at first, he matures through the journey, showing courage and wisdom as a new-generation hero.
